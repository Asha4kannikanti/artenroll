1.  cd Application
2.  cd ..
3. pwd (present working directry)
4. ls   (list)
5. ls -a  (To check hidden files)
6. ls -lrt (to check permission)
7. mkdir foldername(make directory)
8. touch filename(create a file)
9. rmdir foldername (remove directory) only empty files
10. rm -rf foldername/filename (delete file/folder)
